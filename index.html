About Me

Hi, I'm Paul J Reed II, a 35-year-old author born in Weymouth, MA, and now residing in Plymouth, MA. I've always been a fan of sci-fi movies and fantasy shows. Influenced by shows like Stargate, Star Trek, Star Wars, and Firefly, I embarked on my writing journey. My first book, Stellar Horizons, started as short versions and sections before I combined and refined them into a full novel.
